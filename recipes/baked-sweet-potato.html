Baked Sweet Potatoes

Description

These baked sweet potato cubes, seasoned with olive oil and oregano, make a delicious side dish. The whole family enjoys eating sweet potato when I make this recipe!

These baked sweet potato cubes will pair perfectly with all your favorite seasonal dinners.

You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 large sweet potatoes
  • 2 pinches dried oregano or to taste
  • 2 pinches salt
  • 2 pinches ground black pepper
