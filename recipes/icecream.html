Georgia Peach Homemade Ice Cream

Description

This peach ice cream recipe is the best you'll ever eat! If you prefer ice cream on the sweeter side, you may use up to 1 cup sugar in this recipe. Chilling the container first will help the ice cream harden faster.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ pounds fresh peaches - peeled, pitted and chopped
  • 1 pint half-and-half cream
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 1 (14 ounce) can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups whole milk, or as needed

Steps

  1. Working in batches, purée peaches with half-and-half and sugar in a blender or food processor.
  2. Mix together peach mixture, sweetened condensed milk, evaporated milk, and vanilla in a gallon ice cream freezer container. Pour enough whole milk into the container to reach the fill line, about 2 cups.
  3. Freeze ice cream according to the manufacturer's instructions.
